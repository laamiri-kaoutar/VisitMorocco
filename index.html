About morocco

capital

Rabat is the political capital, while Casablanca is the largest city and economic hub.

Language

Arabic is the official language, with Berber also recognized. French is widely spoken.

Currency

The official currency is the Moroccan Dirham (MAD).

Landscapes

Morocco offers diverse landscapes including the Atlas Mountains, Sahara Desert, and Mediterranean and Atlantic coastlines.

Cuisine

Famous for dishes like couscous, tagine, and mint tea.

Tourisme

Top destinations include Marrakech, Fes, Chefchaouen, and the Sahara for cultural and adventure tourism.
